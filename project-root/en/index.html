Construction services

Partition wall masonry

We build new partition walls, for example from brick or blocks, to divide spaces. The work includes measurement, masonry, and surface finishing.

Suspended ceilings

We install suspended ceiling systems (e.g., plasterboard or tile grid) to create a clean finish and hide wiring or pipes.

Walls

Wall construction and boarding (e.g., plasterboard), leveling, and preparation for painting or other surface finishes.

Frames

Construction of supporting frame structures (wood or metal) for buildings or walls, onto which boards and other materials are installed.

Renovation

Repairing or renewing existing spaces. Can include demolition, repairs, and installation of new structures.

Door installation

Installation of a new door and frame, adjustment, and verification that the door operates properly and closes tightly.

Window installation

Replacement of old windows or installation of new ones, sealing and finishing to ensure insulation and proper operation.

About

Local team, clear execution

Founded in 2022 and based in Valkeakoski, MRH Rakennus Oy is a Finnish construction company specializing in high-quality new construction, renovation projects, and sustainable infrastructure development.

We differentiate ourselves through strong professional expertise, responsible operations, and a customer-oriented approach. Every project is carefully planned and executed with precision and on schedule.

Our team consists of motivated professionals who continuously develop their skills and are committed to delivering excellence. We are not just getting our work done - we are building trust, safety, and a sustainable future for our clients.
