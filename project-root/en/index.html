About

Local team, clear execution

Founded in 2022 and based in Valkeakoski, MRH Rakennus Oy is a Finnish construction company specializing in high-quality new construction, renovation projects, and sustainable infrastructure development.

We differentiate ourselves through strong professional expertise, responsible operations, and a customer-oriented approach. Every project is carefully planned and executed with precision, on schedule, and within agreed budgets.

Our team consists of motivated professionals who continuously develop their skills and are committed to delivering excellence. We are not just getting our work done - we are building trust, safety, and a sustainable future for our clients.
